About TeamSync

TeamSync is a free, open-source tool designed to help you quickly and fairly create random teams for any purpose. Whether you're organizing a sports event, classroom activity, or work project, TeamSync ensures balanced and unbiased team distribution.

Our algorithm randomly shuffles participants while respecting your specified team count, providing you with perfectly balanced teams every time.

TeamSync is completely free to use with no ads or tracking. The code is open-source and available on GitHub for anyone to review or contribute.
